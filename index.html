Welcome to City Snapshot

City Snapshot provides information about interesting cities around the world. You will find the overall quality of life score (out of 100), specific scores (out of 10) for aspects of that city, such as cost of living, environmental quality, leisure & culture, etc., as well as today's weather, and articles related to the city. By clicking the start button below, City Snapshot will randomly choose a city for you to explore. From there you can continue generating random cities, or choose a city from the list.

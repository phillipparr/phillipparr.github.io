Welcome to City Snapshot

City Snapshot provides information about interesting cities around the world. Here you will find the overall quality of life score (/100), specific scores (/10) for aspects of that city, such as cost of living, environmental quality, leisure & culture, etc., today's weather, and the top six news stories about that city. By clicking the start button below, City Snapshot will randomly generate an interesting city for you to explore. From there, you can continue generating random cities, or choose a city from the list.
